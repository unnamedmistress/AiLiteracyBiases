üß† Real-Life Setup

It's Monday. You have 47 support tickets. Half are "it's not working" with zero details. One is "I DEMAND A REFUND" in all caps. Another is a 500-word essay about someone's weekend.

You need AI to help triage this chaos.

But "summarize this ticket" isn't going to cut it. You need advanced prompting techniques: few-shot learning, chain-of-thought reasoning, tool use, and retrieval.

What You'll Learn

  • Few-Shot Prompting: Show the model examples so it learns what "good" looks like
  • Chain-of-Thought (CoT): Ask AI to show its work step-by-step
  • Tool Use & Function Calling: Tell AI how to use real tools (APIs, databases)
  • Retrieval & Grounding: Pull in real facts before asking questions
  • Safety & Constraints: Know when to say "I can't help with that"
