Chain-of-Thought (CoT)

Force the model to reason step-by-step, then verify before final answers.

CoT patterns

  • Thoughts vs answers: separate channels.
  • Ask for alternatives, then choose.
  • Add a verification step: "Double-check for math & sources."

Quick check

See if you can spot solid CoT prompts.

1) Why separate thoughts from answers?

2) What closes CoT loops safely?
