Workflow building blocks

Inputs ‚Üí model ‚Üí tools ‚Üí human review ‚Üí outputs.

Patterns

  • Inputs: structured fields, files, or URLs; sanitize + validate early.
  • AI steps: prompting templates, CoT, tool calls, and retrieval for facts.
  • Human-in-the-loop: approvals for high-risk or ambiguous cases.
  • Outputs: typed schemas (JSON), post-checks, and logging.
