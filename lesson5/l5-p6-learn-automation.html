Automation & orchestration

Triggers, queues, retries, and tool calls that keep flows moving.

Patterns

  • Triggers: webhooks, schedulers, or inbox rules that start the flow.
  • Queues: decouple slow steps; process in workers with backpressure.
  • Retries/fallbacks: exponential backoff, circuit breakers, cached responses.
  • Tooling: connectors (Zapier/Make), actions/plugins, and guarded APIs.
