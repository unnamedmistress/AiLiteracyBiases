Plain-Language Rules

  • Don't share private info (PII, passwords, confidential data)
  • Don't copy someone else's content without permission
  • Always cite facts or sources
  • Use disclaimers if needed (e.g. "AI-generated")

Why It Matters

Real Examples of What Not to Do:
  • Chatbot blurbs that leak personal data
  • Bot posting unverified health or legal advice
  • AI-generated content that plagiarizes existing work

Guardrail Matrix

When What to Do
Before (input) Filter private or sensitive data
During (process) Limit what AI can output
After (output) Validate content, check for plagiarism
Post-run (log & review) Record what was generated, who approved it
