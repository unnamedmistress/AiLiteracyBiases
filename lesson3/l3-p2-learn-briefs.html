Scenario Setup

You're about to ask AI to write a tweet about "work-life balance tips."

But you only type: "Write a tweet about work-life balance."

It replies: "Work-life balance helps you relax."

Meh. That's the kind of tweet that gets 2 likes (one from your mom, one from a bot).

Instead, if you tell it who the audience is, what tone, what vibe ‚Äî it already feels more like you wrote it yourself.

Why a Good Brief Matters

  • Avoid vague or meaningless output ‚Äî no more "synergy" and "leverage"
  • Control tone, length, and style upfront ‚Äî not after 5 rounds of edits
  • Save time ‚Äî less editing later means more time for doom-scrolling
